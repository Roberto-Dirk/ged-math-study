# Unit 4: Data & Probability

## Overview

This unit covers statistics, data analysis, and probability. You'll learn to interpret graphs, calculate averages, and understand likelihood.

## Topics Covered

### 4.1 Data Representation

**Key Concepts:**
- Organizing data
- Reading tables and charts
- Bar graphs, line graphs, and pie charts
- Histograms and box plots

### 4.2 Measures of Central Tendency

**Key Concepts:**
- Mean (average)
- Median (middle value)
- Mode (most frequent value)
- Range

**Example:**
Data set: 2, 5, 7, 7, 10
- Mean = $\frac{2+5+7+7+10}{5} = 6.2$
- Median = 7
- Mode = 7
- Range = $10 - 2 = 8$

### 4.3 Probability

**Key Concepts:**
- Basic probability
- Theoretical vs. experimental probability
- Compound probability
- Permutations and combinations

**Formula:**
$$P(\text{event}) = \frac{\text{favorable outcomes}}{\text{total outcomes}}$$

### 4.4 Statistics and Analysis

**Key Concepts:**
- Standard deviation
- Correlation and causation
- Interpreting statistical data
- Making predictions from data

### 4.5 Trends and Relationships

**Key Concepts:**
- Linear relationships
- Scatterplots
- Lines of best fit
- Interpreting trends
