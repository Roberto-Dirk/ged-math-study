# 🎓 GED Math Study Platform

Welcome to your comprehensive, interactive study guide for **GED Mathematics**!

This platform is designed to help you master all four units of GED math through structured lessons, interactive practice, engaging games, and personalized study plans.

## What's Included

### 📚 Content
Structured lessons covering all GED math units:
- **Unit 1**: Number and Operations
- **Unit 2**: Algebra
- **Unit 3**: Geometry
- **Unit 4**: Data & Probability

### 🎯 Interactive Practice
- **Quizzes** with instant feedback
- **Flashcards** for quick review
- **Problem Sets** organized by difficulty
- **Educational Games** to reinforce learning

### 📋 Study Tools
- **Structured Study Plans** - Week-by-week schedules
- **Video Tutorials** - Key concepts explained
- **Downloadable PDFs** - Worksheets and study materials

## Getting Started

1. **Choose a unit** from the Content section
2. **Work through lessons** and take notes
3. **Practice with quizzes** and flashcards
4. **Follow your study plan** for consistent progress
5. **Track your improvement** with problem sets
