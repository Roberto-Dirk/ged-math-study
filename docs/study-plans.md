# 📅 Study Plans

Structured study plans help you progress systematically through GED math. Choose a plan based on your timeline!

## 📆 8-Week Intensive Plan

**Best for**: Students with 8 weeks to prepare

### Week 1-2: Unit 1 Foundations
- **Unit 1 Lessons**: Number Operations (3 hours)
- **Daily practice**: 30 minutes quizzes
- **Flashcards**: 10 minutes daily
- **Checkpoint**: Unit 1 practice test

### Week 3-4: Unit 2 Algebra
- **Unit 2 Lessons**: Algebra basics (3 hours)
- **Daily practice**: 30 minutes problem sets
- **Games**: 15 minutes daily
- **Checkpoint**: Unit 2 practice test

### Week 5-6: Unit 3 Geometry
- **Unit 3 Lessons**: Geometry fundamentals (3 hours)
- **Daily practice**: 30 minutes quizzes
- **Visualization**: 15 minutes with diagrams
- **Checkpoint**: Unit 3 practice test

### Week 7-8: Unit 4 + Review
- **Unit 4 Lessons**: Data & Probability (3 hours)
- **Comprehensive review**: All units (2 hours)
- **Full-length exam**: 3 hours
- **Final review**: Weak areas

---

## 📆 12-Week Balanced Plan

**Best for**: Students with 12 weeks and moderate experience

### Weeks 1-3: Unit 1
- Monday/Wednesday/Friday: Lessons & lectures (1.5 hours)
- Tuesday/Thursday: Problem sets (1 hour)
- Daily: Flashcards (10 minutes)
- Weekend: Review & games (1 hour)

### Weeks 4-6: Unit 2
- Same schedule as Unit 1
- Focus on: Understanding concepts deeply

### Weeks 7-9: Unit 3
- Same schedule
- Emphasis on: Visualization

### Weeks 10-12: Unit 4 + Comprehensive Review
- Week 10: Unit 4 content
- Week 11: Integrated practice across all units
- Week 12: Final exam prep & review

---

## 📆 16-Week Comprehensive Plan

**Best for**: Beginners or those wanting thorough mastery

### Weeks 1-4: Unit 1
- **Monday-Friday**: Structured lessons (1 hour)
- **Daily practice**: 20 minutes
- **Weekly assessment**: Mini-quizzes

### Weeks 5-8: Unit 2
- Same pace as Unit 1
- Deeper dive into advanced topics

### Weeks 9-12: Unit 3
- Same pace
- Extra focus on 3D geometry

### Weeks 13-14: Unit 4
- Gentle introduction to statistics
- Extra time for probability concepts

### Weeks 15-16: Comprehensive Review
- Full-length practice exams
- Targeted review of weak areas

---

## ✅ How to Stay on Track

1. **Set a study schedule** and treat it like an appointment
2. **Use a planner** to mark completed units
3. **Track your scores** to see improvement
4. **Take breaks** - 5 minutes per 25 minutes of study
5. **Stay consistent** - Even 20 minutes daily beats cramming
6. **Adjust as needed** - Spend extra time on hard topics
