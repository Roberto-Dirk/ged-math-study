# About This Platform

## Welcome to GED Math Study Platform

This comprehensive, free platform is designed to help students prepare for the GED Mathematics test through interactive lessons, practice problems, and engaging learning tools.

## Our Mission

We believe that **every student deserves access to quality education**. This platform makes GED math preparation:
- ✅ **Free** - No subscription or hidden costs
- ✅ **Accessible** - Available on any device
- ✅ **Interactive** - Engaging quizzes, games, and flashcards
- ✅ **Comprehensive** - Covers all 4 GED math units
- ✅ **Flexible** - Study at your own pace

## What's Included

### 📚 Complete Content
- Detailed lessons for all 4 GED units
- Visual explanations and examples
- Real-world applications
- Video tutorials

### 🎯 Interactive Practice
- Instant feedback quizzes
- Customizable problem sets
- Flashcard decks
- Educational games

### 📋 Study Tools
- Structured study plans (8, 12, and 16-week options)
- Progress tracking
- Downloadable worksheets
- Formula reference sheets

### 📊 Assessment
- Practice tests
- Unit assessments
- Performance analytics
- Personalized recommendations

## How to Get Started

1. **Choose a study plan** based on your timeline
2. **Start with Unit 1** or review specific topics
3. **Follow the recommended sequence**: Lessons → Quizzes → Practice → Games
4. **Track your progress** as you advance
5. **Take full practice tests** to assess readiness

## Who This Is For

This platform is ideal for:
- Students preparing for the GED exam
- Adult learners returning to education
- Non-native English speakers building math skills
- Anyone needing to refresh their math fundamentals
